A True Terror

The sasquatch is a massive, furry, killing machine. Just look at that thing. She'll rip your frickin' head off. Sasquatch is female, thirty feet tall, fourty feet wide, has a confirmed kill count of 30, and an estimated kill count in the hundreds. Found in the northwestern United States, the locals commonly refer to it as "Your Mom."

It has long, serrated claws, is hideously mangy, and can eat bacon by the metric ton. It typically preys on the local sailor bars, or the dumpsters outside.

Sasquatch is rumored to have a lifespan in the hundreds of years. However, due to her poor diet, sedentary lifestyle, and rampant alcoholism, this appraisal is likely quite high. Sasquatch has an estimated several hundred offspring, all from different fathers, all of which are considered evolutionary dead-ends.
